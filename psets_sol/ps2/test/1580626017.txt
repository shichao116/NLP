Romantic Antics: Creative ideas For Successful First Dates, Adventurous Saturday Nights, And Playful Long Weekends is a handy and &quot;user friendly&quot; self-help guide to keeping male/female relationships passionate, adventurous, and close. Written by Joy Decker and Kevin Decker (a happily married husband-and-wife duo and creators of the successful InspriationPoint.com web site), Romantic Antics offers such ideas as learning to practice and enjoy massage, rekindling dating fervor, romantic recipes, new ways to celebrate holidays together, and much, much more. Romantic Antics is warmly recommended as an inspirational treasure trove of ideas of kindling (and rekindling) romance
